Located on Belvoir Avenue, off Wilkinson Road, one of the most prestigious and secure addresses in Freetown, our property offers the ideal setting for short stays, extended visits, and yearly residency.

Backed by over a decade of experience in Sierra Leone's real estate industry, we blend modern sophistication with the kind of warm, personalised service that makes every guest feel truly at home.

04 · Dining

A World of
Flavour

Our on-site restaurant celebrates two traditions: the vibrant, generous flavours of African cuisine, and refined Continental favourites, open for breakfast through dinner.

From à la carte breakfast to candlelit dinners, our kitchen crafts every meal with locally sourced ingredients and international technique. Breakfast is included with studio rooms, one-bedroom apartments, penthouses, and ground floor rooms. Custom meal preparation is available on request.
