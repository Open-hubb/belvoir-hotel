## 2. Property Description (Official / Self-Described)

Belvoir is a collection of **newly built (2020 & 2023)**, fully furnished, self-contained luxury Hotel Rooms, Studios, 1-bedroom and 2-bedroom Serviced Apartments and Residences. Located on Belvoir Avenue, off Wilkinson Road — one of the most popular and secured areas in Freetown — the property is ideal for short stays, long stays, and yearly residence.

The apartments boast **breathtaking panoramic views** of the exotic Lumley and Aberdeen beaches and creeks, making it a true "home away from home" in the heart of Freetown.

The property is backed by **over 10 years of experience** in the Sierra Leone real estate industry, with a reputation for warm, charming, and friendly hospitality.
